Fimero

Companion site to Software Engineering in Finance



Our aim to to provide a suite of services that give students exposure to real-world software engineering practices using free, open source tools. By using free and open source tools, we achieve two important goals for the students: we prepare them with tools they can keep after they complete the program and we encourage them to tinker to better understand their workflow.

    Repository

    We use Mercurial, a free, distributed source control system written in Python and C.
